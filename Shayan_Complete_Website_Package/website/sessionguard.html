SessionGuard

A session intelligence platform that helps users track, analyze, and improve decision-making patterns through data, reports, and risk insights.

Product build / V10.1 wiring milestoneTurn every session into intelligence.

Audience

Users who want personal analytics, reports, risk tags, and decision pattern review without gambling advice or outcome promises.

Problem

People often make repeated decisions without clear records, behavior summaries, or pattern visibility.

Solution

SessionGuard converts uploads or live session data into dashboards, comparisons, alerts, reports, and behavior analytics focused on discipline and awareness.

Recommended website CTA

Position as a responsible analytics product, not a gaming recommendation tool.

Core features / sections

  • CSV upload and session processing
  • Live capture and WebSocket updates
  • Comparison reports and trend analysis
  • Risk scoring and behavior tags
  • PDF exports and visual reports
  • Desktop proof track planned
