# Website Builder Brief

## Goal
Build a full multi-page website, not a single landing page. The site should present Shayan's ecosystem of AI products, business ventures, media brands, marketing systems, and service businesses. Each project/business must have its own dedicated page.

## Target website type
A premium venture ecosystem website that can work as:
- portfolio hub
- product/project showcase
- business/service website
- investor/client explainer
- future launch platform
- project archive

## Design direction
Premium, modern, dark, cinematic, professional, operator-grade. It should feel like a mix of a serious AI lab, venture studio, and product company. Avoid cheap templates, clutter, childish visuals, or generic startup filler.

## Required structure
1. Home
2. Projects
3. Businesses
4. Services
5. About
6. Contact
7. Privacy
8. Terms
9. Individual page for each project/business

## Required project pages
- Agent Command Center (ACC): A modular AI operating system that routes goals across agents, tools, approvals, memory, automation, and connected workflows.
- Alphonso Ecosystem: A cinematic local-first AI ecosystem where specialized agents collaborate inside a premium desktop command environment.
- SessionGuard: A session intelligence platform that helps users track, analyze, and improve decision-making patterns through data, reports, and risk insights.
- TapCash: A GPT-style rewards and offerwall platform concept focused on lean launch, attribution, anti-fraud, and scalable reward operations.
- Obsidian Media: A media and content strategy brand for social campaigns, storytelling systems, YouTube/Instagram workflows, and AI-assisted content operations.
- Obsidian Studios: A creative studio brand for visuals, trailers, faceless channels, AI cinematics, brand kits, and campaign assets.
- Blazely: A commission-based marketing and landing-page system for lead generation, business growth, and automated campaign workflows.
- Founder Project: A systemized AI company-in-a-box concept that helps founders move from idea to offer, operations, content, automations, and launch assets.
- Cullinan Construction: A general contracting and construction services brand for renovations, new builds, landscaping, project management, and high-quality execution in Ontario.
- AI Tourist Assistant Platform: A tourist assistant concept for major events such as the FIFA World Cup in Canada, helping visitors navigate local services, food, transport, events, and emergency information.
- UniUni DSP Opportunity: A delivery service partner opportunity page for evaluating startup requirements, capital needs, operating model, vehicles, hiring, insurance, and profitability.

## Core requirements
- Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- Clear navigation.
- Each project has its own URL and page.
- Each page has a hero section, audience/problem/solution, features, CTA, and next-step notes.
- Add contact funnel and form backend.
- Add analytics.
- Add SEO metadata and Open Graph images.
- Add real screenshots, demos, or visuals when available.
- Legal pages must be reviewed before launch.

## Suggested tech options
- Fastest no-code: Webflow, Framer, Wix Studio, Squarespace.
- AI builder: Lovable, Bolt, Replit, Cursor, v0.
- Developer build: Next.js + Tailwind + CMS.
- Simple static deployment: current HTML package to Netlify/Vercel/Cloudflare Pages.
