# Brand Direction

## Working brand name
Shayan Venture Ecosystem

## Brand personality
- premium
- strategic
- technical
- cinematic
- execution-focused
- trustworthy
- ambitious but grounded

## Visual direction
- dark interface
- glass panels
- blue/purple/gold accents
- sharp typography
- clean cards
- strong contrast
- technical but not cold

## Avoid
- cheap template feel
- excessive emojis
- unrealistic claims
- vague AI buzzwords
- too many colors
- fake metrics
- unfinished legal/contact pages

## Suggested future assets
- master ecosystem logo
- sub-brand logos
- project screenshots
- agent character visuals
- founder photo
- product demo thumbnails
- Open Graph social cards
